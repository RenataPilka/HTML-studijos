About Project

This project aims to revolutionize the materials industry by developing eco-friendly bionanocomposites as a sustainable alternative to petroleum-based plastics. Using banana plant agricultural waste, we'll isolate cellulosic fibers, nano-cellulose, and nano-lignin, incorporating them into biopolymers like starch, chitosan, sodium alginate, and carrageenan. Our goal is to optimize properties through surface treatments for versatile applications, including food packaging and laboratory equipment.
